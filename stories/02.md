Each word leaving her mouth leaves a trail in the air, lingering for just a heartbeat before dissolving into silver mist, each puff proving that she is, indeed, human, even though her ethereal presence seems to indicate otherwise. 
The cold that makes her breath puff does not leave a trace on her skin, no redness from being exposed to the cold anywhere to be seen. 
"It's cold. Are you cold? You tend to get cold easily when you're like this." 
She digs around in a small bag slung over her shoulder for a moment, before pulling out a pair of brown arm warmers and handing them over. "Here. put these on."
